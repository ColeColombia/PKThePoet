Love is a verb

And so love on it's own is a verb Of so even the laziest man fails
to not work hard and run crazily to prove himself
He's blind cannot see how dump he has become
Even after being told
His tears are not of sadness but love cherishing out of his deep soul
He's not a thief
And with no reason to look over his shoulders
He does for the other with excellent service
He does for the other beyond limit
He does for the other with no care of who passed this road before him
As he's is in good intention
Of not only to prove to the beloved
But to feed too his almost died soul in disbelief of love
He flies unknowingly
And at a surprise he too fails to recognize himself
Though that doesn't bother him
As long as his beloved smiles and recognizes this stranger who lives to progress the heart of her
He is not tired ever
Every morning plants another flower
And yet another till there's no land
He spends on his last fortunes to see smile on hers
Because even if he hungers that's how his soul feeds
That's how his soul fulls
And all that is enough
He loves every second
And reminds every second of his love
Because though he loses and lost himself
His fear still remains

Who will he be if he woke up without her
Who would he be if his words can't be told to her
Who would he become if not a big truck to help her carry her burdens
Who would he be if no longer a warm shelter for her heart
So I still say love is a verb
For so even the clueless man finds a way to work around it
That he does not seek a manual
As he does his absolute best
For he's clueless but does not wait to be reminded
So he works his best and hopes it works
Just so she stays and rests
Love on it's own is a verb
For even the most strongest man that can fight
Puts down his swords
Gets out of his own ring
And learns how to fight
Not for money but her heart
He outlearns his ways of fight
As his heart gentles down to this sweet melody of her face
He bows down at everyones surprise to please her without remorse
And so love is a doing word❤️.
